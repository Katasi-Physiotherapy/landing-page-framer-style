## The Power of Location: Pinpointing Your Shoulder Pain

When shoulder pain strikes at night, t**he exact spot** of discomfort is a vital clue for diagnosis. Our orthopedic physiotherapist experts often emphasize, *"where the pain is, it's very important because that is what will help us to determine the type of cause or the type of injury that you have on your shoulder."*

Consider these questions:

1. Is the pain on the front side of your shoulder? This could indicate issues with your biceps tendon or the front part of your rotator cuff.
